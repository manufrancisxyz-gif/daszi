About daszi

Kerala-based composer and music producer. daszi creates fresh, rich-sounding music that weaves together folk and traditional sounds with contemporary production — bridging cultures through immersive sonic experiences.

From archiving the musical heritage of forests like Attappadi and Karuvanchal to collaborating with artists across the Indian underground, daszi's work sits at the intersection of experimental, ambient, psychedelic, and folktronic music. Live performances, music videos, and visualizers amplify every emotion woven into the sound.
