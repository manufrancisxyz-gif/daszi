About

Vishnu Das (daszi)

Vishnu Das is a composer, producer, and sonic storyteller from Kerala, India. Known for blending Indian folk traditions with contemporary electronic and techno-driven atmospheres, he has garnered over 60,000 monthly listeners on Spotify and a growing global audience.

Rooted in the sounds of Carnatic, Hindustani, and regional Indian folk music, his work bridges cultures through immersive compositions shaped by travels and field recordings. His creative process involves capturing natural and human soundscapes — from the rhythms of rural Kerala to ambient textures of global environments — transforming them into richly layered musical narratives.

Beyond studio work, daszi elevates his artistry through live performances, cinematic visuals, and experimental storytelling. His latest album, "Ft. Nature," celebrates the raw, organic connection between humanity, culture, and the natural world.
